Mission

We believe in the
power of hiring

Because the potential for people to do something amazing has
everything to do with being in the right role, on the right team,
at the right time. That’s where Greenhouse comes in – our
mission is to make every company great at hiring. Because
when they are,companies have the confidence to dream
bigger, think bolder and take on what’s next.

Becoming great at
hiring is a commitment

When your company chooses to work with Greenhouse, you
are getting more than software. You are investing in the belief
that hiring is the most important factor in your company’s
success. You are gaining the support of a team impassioned
by our mission. And you are empowered by a company
committed to the relentless pursuit of better hiring.

We’re here as your
guide

We’ve helped more than 4000 companies on their journey to
become great at hiring. Along the way, we’ve learned that
they need not only the right hiring tools but also know-how,
guidance and partnership.

  • Resources and guidance for where you are now and where
    you’re going

  • A community so we can learn and grow together

  • Technology partnershipsto make it possible to integrate with
    the best

  • Innovation that creates outcomes you can measure

We are dedicated
to being an inclusive
company

When people are heard, their voices can make a difference.
When people feel included, they bring their whole selves
to their work. And when they feel like they belong, they can be
part of a team that can achieve anything.

And we’ll keep
working to get better

We’re committed to creating a diverse and inclusive
workplace where everyone can be their true selves. Whether
it’s finding candidates in underrepresented groups, using
hiring tools to reduce unconscious bias, or our employee-led
culture committee and special interest groups, we’re putting
our beliefs into practice. But we have more to do, and we’re
continuing to learn and work each day to get better.
